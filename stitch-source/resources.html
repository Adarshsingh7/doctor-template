Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive for my surgery? expand_more

We generally recommend arriving at least 2 hours prior to your scheduled surgical time to complete the admission process and preoperative preparation.

Will I need someone to drive me home? expand_more

Yes, for all neurosurgical procedures, you must have a designated adult driver to take you home as anesthesia and neuro-recovery protocols prohibit operating a vehicle.
